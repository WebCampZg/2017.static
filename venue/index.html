Venue_

WebCamp Zagreb conference is held at the Plaza Event Center (previously Hypo Expo XXI conference center), Slavonska avenija 6, Zagreb

Parking

The conference center has an underground garage which can be accessed from the south and costs 6kn/h. There are many paid parking spaces located in the area around the conference center (Zagreb parking 3rd zone) for 1,5kn/h or 20 kn/day. These spaces are charged on Friday from 7:00 to 20:00 and Saturday from 7:00 to 15:00.
