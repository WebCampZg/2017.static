Amphinicy Technologies

Amphinicy Technologies is a leading provider of complex software solutions and all-around software support for the satellite and space industry.

Being on the market for 18 years we have delivered many successful international projects to our customers, both tailor-made solutions and our own products.

Operating from Luxembourg and Croatia we provide premium quality both in Europe and abroad to international space and humanitarian agencies, satellite operators, teleports and VSAT vendors, RF equipment manufacturers and multimedia and broadband service providers. 

If you are interested in joining our team and developing hi-tech software and satellite systems, send us your CV in English at careers@amphinicy.com and we will be happy to meet you in person. We are recruiting talents at all times!
