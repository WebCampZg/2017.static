Welcome to Zagreb, host city of the WebCamp Zagreb conference for the fifth year in a row. WebCamp Zagreb is a technology oriented conference for developers & designers, which attracted over 800 professionals and entrepreneurs in 2017. and promises to reach similar numbers this year.

Zagreb is the cultural, scientific, economic, political and administrative center of the Republic of Croatia. It is a safe city whose doors are always open and where everything is accessible on foot – from your hotel to the main square or WebCamp Zagreb Conference 2017.

Get to know the city of Zagreb and it's beautiful scenery through movies and images.

If you're travelling to Zagreb from abroad to enter Croatia you will need a valid passport or, for some countries (EU), a personal identity card will be enough.

These links will help you find your way around the capital, especially if you're visiting for the first time:

  • Tourist information center
  • Zagreb Tourist Board
  • Accomodation in Zagreb

If you find yourself in an emergency situation here is a list of the most important telephone numbers:

  • (+385) 112 – emergency number
  • (+385) 192 – police
  • (+385) 193 – fire brigade
  • (+385) 194 – ambulance
  • (+385) 195 – search and rescue at the sea
  • (+385) 1987 – road assistance
  • (+385) 11888 – telephone directory
  • (+385) 18166 – weather forecast

While attending the conference please abide by our Code of Conduct. Have fun and see you in Zagreb!
