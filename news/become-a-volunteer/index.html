Become a volunteer

Be a part of WebCamp Zagreb 2017 conference by applying to volunteer.

WebCamp Zagreb is a community driven conference oriented toward technology that powers today’s web. It is a two day event in Zagreb that will bring together 30+ speakers and 800+ attendees, sharing their expertise, learning, networking and job hunting.

One of the ways to get involved is by becoming a volunteer. Volunteering is fun and allows you to meet new people - speakers, organisers, other volunteers, and of course visitors.

Of course there is actual work to be done. It includes helping us to set up the venue before the conference, preparing everything for our visitors, handling the registration, and taking care of all other details that make the conference and the venue a pleasant place during these two days.

If you wish to be a member of the crew making sure things go smoothly, you're welcome to join us. Please, fill in the application form at and we'll get in touch soon.
